How to play

The computer will generate a word

Reply to that word using the last letter of the previous word

Your word has to be 7 letters long no numbers or special characters

(hit enter to input your word)

The computer generates a new word

Get the highest score you can

You lose if:

A non valid word is introduced (Instant loss)

3 failed words

(shorter than 7 letters)

(Not starting with the last letter of the previous word)
